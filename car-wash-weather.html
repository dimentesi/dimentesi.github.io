What is Car Wash Weather?

Have you ever been caught by a sudden rain next day after car wash? If YES now you can forget about the annoying mud from wet asphalt and puddles on your clean car - free app Car Wash Weather suggests you the most favorable days to visit a car wash based on current weather forecast and shows the nearest carwash station or detailing service in your geo-location. This app is as easy to use as an obvious speedometer â€” just open application and see the favorable car wash days, swipe between screens to see the nearest car washes and relevant weather details and decide on the best date to take care of a car appearance.
New app to your car dashboard: are you using Weather Underground, AccuWeather or Yahoo Weather services for planning your day and Cars.com, GasBuddy, CarBuzz, Torque or Carango to watch after your ride? Include Car Wash Weather to this list! Look for favorable washing day and be a proud owner of your shining ride on a parking or going through city traffic!
